FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

DO YOU DELIVER OUTSIDE MANILA?

Unfortunately, we currently only deliver within Manila. We hope to expand our delivery areas in the future and would love to serve you when that happens.

CAN I ADD SOME SPECIAL REQUESTS TO MY ORDER?

Absolutely! We’d be happy to accommodate your requests for your order. Please let us know the details by specifying it on the additional requests question on the provided Google form when you order, and we’ll do our best to make it happen.

DO YOU OFFER DISCOUNTS FOR LARGE ORDERS?

Yes, we can definitely accommodate discounts for large orders! Please feel free to reach out with the details by contacting us through the contacts found in our website, and we’ll be happy to assist you further.

CAN I CANCEL OR MODIFY MY ORDER AFTER PLACING IT?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your order after placing it, but please let us know as soon as possible so we can accommodate your request before the preparation begins. Feel free to reach out to us, and we’ll be happy to assist you!

WHAT PAYMENT METHODS DO YOU ACCEPT?

At the moment, we only accept CASH ON DELIVERY. However, we’re constantly working to improve, and we hope to offer more payment options in the future!

ARE THE DISHES FRESHLY PREPARED AFTER ORDERING?

Absolutely! All our dishes are freshly prepared upon ordering to ensure you receive the best quality and flavor every time. We take pride in serving food that’s made with care and love.
